Frequently Asked

Questions

Efficient transport starts with the right tools. Our system is here to streamline your operations and ensure smooth, secure fleet management.

1. What is the Aggregate Transport Truck Management System?

Our system is designed to help businesses manage their fleet of trucks efficiently. It allows for streamlined scheduling, GPS tracking, and vehicle attendance tracking, all tailored to meet the unique needs of aggregate transport operations.

2. How does GPS tracking work in the system?

Our system uses GPS devices that send location data (latitude and longitude) every 30 seconds to help track vehicles' approximate locations within an operational area. This helps optimize routing and scheduling without real-time monitoring.

3. What kind of data does the system collect?

We collect business-related information, vehicle details, and location data sent by GPS trackers. Additionally, RFID technology is used for attendance tracking to ensure secure vehicle access and operations.

4. Is the GPS tracking data in real-time?

No, our GPS system sends location data every 30 seconds, providing periodic updates, not real-time tracking.

5. How secure is the data in the system?

We take data security very seriously. All collected information is securely stored using encryption and strict access controls. Only authorized personnel from your company and ours can access the data.

6. How long is the data stored?

Data is retained as long as necessary for operational and legal purposes. We ensure that data is only kept for as long as it is useful for transport management.

7. Can I access the data anytime?

Yes, you can access your vehicle and operational data at any time through our secure system portal. Access is limited to authorized users.

8. How does RFID tracking work in the system?

RFID technology is used to track vehicle attendance. It ensures that vehicles are correctly checked in and out of operational areas, enhancing security and accountability.

9. Do I need to install any additional hardware?

The system requires GPS tracking devices and RFID tags, which will be installed on the vehicles and required access points. These devices are part of the setup to ensure smooth operation.

10. How can I get support if I have an issue?

If you encounter any issues or have questions, our support team is available to assist you. Please contact us through our support page or via the provided contact details on the website.
